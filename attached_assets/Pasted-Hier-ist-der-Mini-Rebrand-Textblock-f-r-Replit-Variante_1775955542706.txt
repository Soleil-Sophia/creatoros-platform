## Mini‑Rebrand Prompt — “CreatorOS by LXST” (Bridge Phase, minimal changes)

Refine the existing **creatoros-site** marketing app to introduce a clean brand hierarchy using the transitional label **“CreatorOS by LXST”**.

### Goal

Introduce LXST as the clear sender brand without renaming the whole project or rewriting the site. Keep CreatorOS as the front-facing product/brand for now.

### Constraints

- Do not redesign the site.
- Do not change routes or page structure.
- Do not introduce new CTAs.
- Keep the visual hierarchy calm and premium: **CreatorOS stays primary**, **by LXST stays subtle**.
- Apply changes consistently across header, footer, about, and OG metadata.

---

### 1) Header (site-wide)

Update the header brand mark so it reads:

- Primary: **CreatorOS**
- Secondary (small/subtle): **by LXST**

Rules:

- “by LXST” must be visually secondary (smaller size, lower contrast, subtle spacing).
- Keep it inline or as a small subline—no extra clutter.

---

### 2) Footer (site-wide)

Update the footer brand line to:

**CreatorOS by LXST**  

*Clarity-first tools for creators and solo brands.*

Rules:

- Keep the footer minimal.
- Do not add additional brand layers or extra logos.
- Keep link structure unchanged.

---

### 3) About page (top / first paragraph)

Add or update the opening line on **/about** to:

**CreatorOS is a product by LXST, built for creators and solo brands who need structure, clarity, and direction.**

Keep the rest of the About page as-is unless small edits are needed for tone consistency.

---

### 4) OG / Social preview titles (brand consistency)

Ensure OG and Twitter metadata stay consistent with the new hierarchy:

- Site name: **CreatorOS**
- Include “by LXST” subtly where appropriate (prefer description over title if titles get too long).

Add/ensure for the default OG card composition:

- Large: **CreatorOS**
- Small: **by LXST**

If a product-specific OG image exists (Creator Clarity Kit), keep:

- Large: **Creator Clarity Kit**
- Small: **CreatorOS by LXST**
- Keep text minimal so it remains legible in small previews.

---

### 5) Final check

- Header shows “CreatorOS” with subtle “by LXST” on every page.
- Footer shows “CreatorOS by LXST” + tagline on every page.
- /about starts with the new line.
- OG previews don’t become cluttered or overly long.
- No new CTAs introduced; no CTA labels changed.
